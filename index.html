How to add our photos

Replace these files with your real images (same names) and push to GitHub:

  • assets/her-1.jpg, assets/her-2.jpg, assets/her-3.jpg
  • assets/her.jpg, assets/mine.jpg and assets/us.jpg
  • Optional gallery: assets/cutie-01.jpg … assets/cutie-30.jpg

Tip: best is .jpg (fastest). If you use .png/.webp, either rename to .jpg or update the filenames in index.html.
